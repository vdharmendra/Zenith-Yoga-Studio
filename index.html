Downward Dog

(Adho Mukha Svanasana)

A classic pose that embodies strength, flexibility, and balance. It can visually represent the foundation of a yoga practice.

Warrior II

(Virabhadrasana II)

A powerful standing pose symbolizing confidence, strength, and focus, perfect for conveying energy and determination.

Tree Pose

(Vrksasana)

This pose represents balance and grounding, visually appealing for promoting mindfulness and stability.

Child's Pose

(Balasana)

A soothing, restorative pose that evokes relaxation, calmness, and a sense of comfort, ideal for inviting newcomers.

Lotus Pose

(Padmasana)

A meditative seated pose associated with peace and tranquility, perfect for highlighting the spiritual and mental aspects of yoga.
